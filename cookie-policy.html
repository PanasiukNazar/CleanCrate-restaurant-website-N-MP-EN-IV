Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website dedicated to healthy food, vegan menu options, and ration delivery services. It describes what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember your preferences, improving your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our website, such as enabling navigation and secure access to certain features.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, helping us improve its performance and usability.
  • Functional Cookies: These remember your preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience, such as language selection or meal preferences.
  • Marketing Cookies: These track your browsing habits to provide relevant promotional content. We do not use marketing cookies that identify you personally.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies enable us to:

  • Facilitate your navigation and secure access to services.
  • Remember your meal preferences and delivery options.
  • Analyze website usage to improve our services and user experience.
  • Provide content tailored to your interests without collecting personally identifiable information.

Your Cookie Choices

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. You may choose to block or delete cookies; however, disabling certain cookies might affect the functionality and usability of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third-party service providers to place cookies on our site for analytics and service enhancement. These third parties are required to respect your privacy and comply with relevant regulations.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our cookie use or legal requirements. Please review it periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Cookie Policy or our use of cookies, please contact us through our official channels. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy.
