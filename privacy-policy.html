Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you interact with our restaurant services focused on healthy food, vegan menu options, and ration delivery. Our aim is to protect your privacy while providing valuable information about our offerings and delivery processes.

Information We Collect

To improve our services and provide you with the best experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Such as your name, contact information, and delivery address needed for order processing and communication.
  • Order and Preference Data: Information related to your food preferences, meal choices, delivery schedules, and feedback.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you interact with our website and services, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Ensure accurate and timely delivery of healthy meals and vegan rations.
  • Tailor meal options and delivery schedules to your preferences.
  • Communicate updates about menu changes, delivery notices, and nutritional information.
  • Enhance our website functionality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal requirements and prevent misuse of services.

Security Measures

We take your privacy seriously and apply strict security protocols to protect your personal data, including:

  • Data encryption during transfer and storage.
  • Regular system security reviews and updates.
  • Controlled access to personal data limited to authorized staff.

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your information. However, to facilitate meal preparation and delivery, we may share data with trusted partners such as:

  • Delivery service providers for order fulfillment.
  • Suppliers and kitchen staff involved in meal preparation.
  • Technology providers supporting online ordering and data management.

All partners are obligated to protect your data according to strict privacy standards.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and collect usage data. You can control cookies via your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request copies of your personal data.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your information.
  • Request deletion of your data where legally permissible.
  • Object to certain data processing activities.

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our official channels. We will respond promptly and in compliance with applicable laws.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations. Retention duration depends on the type of data and regulatory requirements.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed about how your data is protected.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, we will promptly delete it.

International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be processed in locations outside your country. We ensure that any transfers comply with privacy standards equivalent to those outlined here.

Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out to us. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is respected and protected.
